Purple Rewards

Our way of saying thank you. Collect stamps with every purchase and earn free coffee just for being a regular.

How It Works

Simple. Rewarding. Free.

01

Pick Up Your Card

Ask any team member for a Purple Rewards card on your next visit. There's no registration, no app, and no fee. Just a physical stamp card to keep in your wallet.

02

Earn a Stamp Per Purchase

Every time you buy any drink or food item, you earn one stamp. On Fridays, every purchase earns you two stamps — our Double Stamp Friday promotion.

03

Your 10th Is Free

Fill your card with 10 stamps and redeem it for any premium coffee of your choice, completely on us. No restrictions — any drink on the hot or cold menu qualifies.

04

Birthday Bonus

Registered members receive a complimentary coffee on their birthday month. Simply show your ID and loyalty card to claim.

Questions

Frequently Asked

How do I get a loyalty card?

Simply ask any team member at the counter during your next visit. Cards are free and there's no sign-up form required to start collecting stamps.

Does every purchase earn a stamp, or only drinks?

Every purchase earns a stamp — drinks, pastries, and snacks all count. You earn one stamp per transaction, not per item.

Can I use my free coffee on any drink?

Yes. A completed card (10 stamps) can be redeemed for any single drink on our menu — hot or cold, including signature drinks. There's no restriction on which item you choose.

Do stamps expire?

Stamps are valid for 12 months from the date of the first stamp on a card. We encourage you to keep visiting so you never lose progress.

How do I register for the birthday benefit?

Visit our contact page or speak to a team member in-store to register your card with your name and birth month. No other personal information is required.

Can I earn stamps on delivery or pre-orders?

Currently, stamps are only collected for in-store purchases. We're working on extending the programme to pre-orders and delivery — watch this space.
