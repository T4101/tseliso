Office Delivery

Coffee, pastries, and snacks delivered straight to your desk across Maseru CBD. Minimum order M80, free delivery over M150.

The Process

How Office Delivery Works

01

Place Your Order

Call us on +266 2231 2345 or WhatsApp +266 5800 1234 with your order and delivery address. For regular office orders, we can set up a standing arrangement.

02

We Prepare and Pack

Your order is freshly prepared and packed in our eco-friendly containers. Hot drinks are sealed to maintain temperature for up to 30 minutes in transit.

03

Delivered to Your Door

Our rider brings your order directly to your building reception or office floor. Typical delivery time is 20–35 minutes from confirmation.
