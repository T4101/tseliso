Our Story

Where Maseru Comes to Savour Every Sip

Purple Coffee was born in 2019 from a single ambition: to bring world-class coffee culture to the heart of Maseru. What began as a small corner café on Kingsway has grown into Lesotho's most beloved coffee destination.

We source our beans directly from Ethiopian and Rwandan farms, roast fresh weekly, and pair them with pastries baked from scratch every morning. From your first cup to your last, we are here to make every moment extraordinary.
