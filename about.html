Our Story

From a single dream to Maseru's most beloved café — the story of Purple Coffee.

Est. 2019

Where Maseru Comes to Savour Every Sip

Purple Coffee was born in 2019 from a single ambition: to bring world-class specialty coffee culture to the heart of Maseru. What began as a small corner café on Kingsway has grown into Lesotho's most celebrated coffee destination — with professional espresso machines, single-origin beans, and a team of passionate baristas who treat every cup as craft.

We believe great coffee is more than a drink. It's a moment of pause, a reason to connect, a daily ritual worth doing properly. Everything we do — from sourcing to service — is in service of that belief.

Our Journey

Five Years in the Making

The First Cup

Purple Coffee opened its doors on Kingsway Road with two espresso machines, four tables, and an unwavering belief that Maseru deserved exceptional coffee.

2019
2020

The Bakery Begins

We launched our in-house bakery, producing croissants, muffins, and loaves from scratch each morning. The Blueberry Muffin quickly became a city-wide favourite.

Direct Sourcing

We established direct-trade relationships with Ethiopian Yirgacheffe and Rwandan highland farmers, giving us full control of the bean-to-cup journey.

2021
2022

Office Delivery Launch

Purple Coffee expanded beyond its walls with an office delivery service across Maseru CBD, serving corporate clients from LNDC Centre to Finance House.

Purple Rewards Programme

We introduced our loyalty stamp card, rewarding our community of regulars with every visit. Over 3,000 cards issued in the first three months.

2023
2024

Maseru's Most Loved

Recognised as Maseru's highest-rated café with over 12,000 customers served and a 5-star Google rating maintained for 18 consecutive months.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Quality Without Compromise

From bean sourcing to the final pour, we hold every step to the highest standard. We never cut corners on ingredients, training, or equipment.

Community First

Purple Coffee exists because of Maseru. We give back through local hiring, supporting nearby suppliers, and creating a space where everyone feels genuinely welcome.

Ethical Sourcing

We pay fair prices directly to our farming partners in Ethiopia and Rwanda. Sustainability is not a marketing position — it is built into every purchasing decision we make.
